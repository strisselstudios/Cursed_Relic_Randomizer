Cursed Relic Randomizer documentation

Thank you for downloading my relic randomizer.
Its pretty self explanatory, but ill give instructions anyway:


~Spin the mystery box to receive a random Map and combination of relics.

~Tier dropdown menu will let you select from 1 of 4 possible Tiers
	The tiers are as follows:
	-Tier 1: Any combination of relics that will result in tier 1 being achieved 	(e.g. 3 Grim Relics, 1 Grim + 1 Sinister Relic, or 1 Wicked Relic).
	
	-Tier 2: Any combination of relics that will result in tier 2 being achieved 

	-Tier 3: Any combination of relics that will result in tier 3 being achieved 

	-Tier 3 HARD: After enough Relics have been slotted to reach the requirements of tier 3, randomly slot extra relics for a greater challenge.


~Reset button in the top left will reset the state of the box back to closed, though you can just spin the box again and it will automatically reset.

~Configure menu will let you select/deselect whichever maps or relics you do or dont want to see in the pool (I know people are going to most likely disable Lawyer's Pen relic, so thats the main purpose). Just press save, and spin the box!
	~If you want to reset back to having all relics and maps selected for the pool, Just press the reset button in the configure menu before saving. 


~Credits tab in the bottom left lists creators of visual assets
